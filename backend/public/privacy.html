Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 3, 2024

Your Privacy Matters: PR Review Assistant is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address and password (encrypted) when you create an account
  • Subscription Information: Payment details processed securely through Stripe (we do not store credit card information)
  • Custom Settings: Security keywords, preferences, and configuration options you define

1.2 Information We Automatically Collect

  • GitHub Pull Request Data: PR metadata, diff content, file names, and comments from PRs you choose to analyze
  • Usage Data: Number of PR analyses, timestamps, repository names, and PR numbers
  • Extension Data: Browser type, extension version, and error logs for debugging

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your GitHub password or personal access tokens (stored only in your browser)
  • Private repository code (unless you explicitly analyze a PR from that repository)
  • Browsing history outside of GitHub pull request pages
  • Personal messages or communications unrelated to PR analysis

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide AI Analysis: Process PR code and metadata through ChatGPT to generate insights and recommendations
  • User Authentication: Verify your identity and manage your account access
  • Subscription Management: Process payments, manage subscription tiers, and enforce usage limits
  • Service Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve features and user experience
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraudulent activity, abuse, and security violations
  • Communication: Send service updates, security alerts, and subscription notifications

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Data Storage

  • User Data: Stored in secure databases with encryption at rest
  • PR Analysis Data: Temporarily processed for analysis and not permanently stored
  • Authentication Tokens: Stored locally in your browser using Chrome's secure storage API

3.2 Security Measures

  • All data transmitted over HTTPS with TLS encryption
  • Passwords hashed using industry-standard bcrypt algorithm
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls and authentication for all API endpoints
  • SOC 2 Type II compliance for Enterprise tier customers

3.3 Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active and for 30 days after account deletion
  • Usage Statistics: Retained for analytics purposes (anonymized after 90 days)
  • PR Analysis Data: Not permanently stored; processed in real-time only

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect and process your data:

4.1 OpenAI (ChatGPT)

  • Processes PR code and metadata to generate AI analysis
  • Subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy
  • Data sent to OpenAI is not used to train their models

4.2 Stripe

  • Processes subscription payments and manages billing
  • Subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy
  • We do not store your credit card information

4.3 GitHub API

  • Retrieves PR data from repositories you have access to
  • Subject to GitHub's Privacy Policy
  • Uses your GitHub authentication for access control

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: OpenAI, Stripe, and infrastructure providers necessary to operate the service
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with user notification)
  • Security Threats: To protect against fraud, abuse, or security vulnerabilities

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

  • Access Your Data: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Update Information: Modify your account details and preferences at any time
  • Delete Account: Permanently delete your account and associated data
  • Export Data: Download your usage statistics and analytics (Pro/Team tiers)

6.2 Privacy Controls

  • Choose which PRs to analyze (opt-in for each analysis)
  • Disable analytics tracking in extension settings
  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails (account notifications still required)

6.3 Browser Permissions

The extension requests the following browser permissions:

  • storage: Store authentication tokens and preferences locally
  • activeTab: Detect GitHub PR pages and inject analysis UI
  • scripting: Display analysis results on GitHub pages
  • github.com: Access PR data for analysis

You can revoke these permissions at any time through Chrome's extension settings.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies and tracking technologies:

  • Essential Cookies: Session authentication and security (required for functionality)
  • Analytics: Usage statistics to improve the service (can be disabled in settings)
  • No Third-Party Tracking: We do not use advertising or tracking pixels

8. Children's Privacy

PR Review Assistant is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy with a new "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an email notification to your registered email address
  • Displaying a notice in the extension for 30 days after the change

Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

12. GDPR Compliance (European Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent

Legal Basis for Processing: We process your data based on:

  • Your consent (for optional features)
  • Contract performance (to provide the service)
  • Legitimate interests (to improve and secure the service)
  • Legal obligations (to comply with applicable laws)
